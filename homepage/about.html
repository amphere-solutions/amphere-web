ABOUT US

VISION

To create an environment where charging will be a handy amenity regardless of space and time, with people not having to worry about their low battery levels.

HOW?

We want to achieve this by providing portable and easy to handle charging facilities at restaurants and cafés across the city. This will be attained by using ready to plug-in power banks for both Android and iPhone users.

THE NEED

In today’s time, people are connected to their phones at every level, so much so that mobiles have become indispensable. From work to pleasure, from calling family to networking with the world, people use their phones for every other thing. No matter where one is, the sign of a low battery is always stressful. But these low battery foes are going to be a thing of past soon. People will find the solution at their doorstep. With no provision of a public charging facility in the city, Amphere partners with cafes which are willing to offer a unique experience to their customers, bringing greater satisfaction. This will very likely create a greater goodwill of the restaurant as it will be catering to the needs of the tech savvy youth.

Amphere will not just provide a service but a better experience at your restaurant. A happy customer likely to come back again and spread a good word in the market. Surveys have shown that people are likely to spend around 30% more time in the restaurant and order more if they have a charging facility to avail. This means one in every four consumers looks for restaurants that can promise readily available charging facility.

A provision like this builds a likeability and eventually a sense of trust within the consumer for the café. They keep returning to the place as they expect similar enjoyable and comfortable experiences. With our service, we will target the broader needs of your consumer base and ensure that a pleasant experience is built not simply in terms of food but also the technological facilities.

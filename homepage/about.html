ABOUT US

VISION

To create an environment where charging will be a handy amenity regardless of space and time, with people not having to worry about their low battery levels.

WHY?

We at Amphere understand that with the indispensability of mobile phones the need to maintain their battery levels has become equally essential. No matter where one is, operating one’s phone on the low battery is stressful. And with the heaping piles of functions that we’ve assigned to our phones, it is quite obvious that they require a fair share of power. However, despite how cautiously we charge our devices, things can always end in the definition of a modern disaster – a dead phone! So, what do you do when you need a backup in the middle of the city? Do you treasure hunt for a charging point or you log into the barely alive mode of your mobile and hope it doesn’t die on you?

WHAT?

We are here to change these dynamics. These low battery foes are going to be a thing of past soon. Now your phone has its precious back up near you, so you no longer need to embarrassingly go about asking friends or strangers for favours. Be it an important call at home or some other urgent work on your phone, you can handle it all with our assistance. Just visit the nearest Amphere partnered café/restaurant near you and you have the solution to your low battery problems through our charging facility. Now you can easily manage the battery levels even outside your home!

HOW?

Step 1: Visit the nearest Amphere partnered café/restaurant.

Step 2: Ask for Amphere.

Step 3: Tada!!! You have it with you. Charge your phone and reconnect with your world again with a prolonged battery life.
